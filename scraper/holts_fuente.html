Arturo Fuente Gran Reserva is a nationwide bestseller. Handmade in the Dominican Republic in the Fuente family factory, these cigars are rolled using a fantastic assortment of aged Dominican tobaccos. The Natural version showcases a gorgeous chocolaty Cameroon wrapper, while the Maduro version dons a dark and delicious Connecticut Broadleaf wrapper. Arturo Fuente cigars are rooted in a rich 3-generation family tradition dating back to the olden days of Cuba. The Fuente family has perfected the art of cigar making, for their cigars are remarkably consistent and wonderfully enjoyable every single time!
